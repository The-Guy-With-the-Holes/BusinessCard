Mould Issues

Our Prior issues with mould:

We have had a lot of issues with mould in our home. It started in the bedroom, where we noticed silk like substances on the walls. At first, we thought it was just spiderwebs, but when we tried to clean it, we realized it was mould. We tried to remove it with bleach, and it seemed to work.

Our previous mould cleaner deterimed that the mould originated from the bathroom (which is shares a wall the bedroom) and that the mould was caused by a leak in the bathroom plumbing.

Our current mould issues:

  1. Mould on exterior roofing garage, front-porch and gutters
  2. Mould on childrens bedroom window (now cleaned)
  3. Mould on the bathroom tiles (now cleaned)
